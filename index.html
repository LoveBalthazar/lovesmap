Cities In New Orleans, Louisiana

Metairie (/meh·tr·ee/) Metairie is located in eastern Jefferson Parish and is bordered by New Orleans to the east. Kenner to the west, Lake Pontchartrain to the north, and the Illinois Central Railroad tracks to the south. South of the railroad are River Ridge, Harahan, Elmwood, and Jefferson.
